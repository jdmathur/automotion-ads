  • Automotion Ads

    AutomotionAds is the fastest growing company in transit media space. Company has largest Autorickshaw-Ad network in India, having 20,000+ autos under the umbrella offering in more than 50 tier-ll and lll cities. Started by passionate young entrepreneurs to change the face of auto-rickshaw industry by offering organised advertisement on auto-rickshaws and working for the welfare of the Auto-rickshaw drivers' community. Won many accolades on national and international level for the unique socio-economic business model.
